11

Voice & copy

Do

  • Plain SA English. "Come to a complete stop."
  • Name actions by outcome: Unlock full access, not Submit payment.
  • Errors say what happened + how to fix it.
  • Encouraging, never patronising: "Getting close — keep going."

Don't

  • No jargon or system words ("webhook", "ITN", "payload").
  • No invented legal/safety claims — content is verified only.
  • No subscription-trap framing — pricing is once-off.
  • No fake urgency or guilt.
